What is Steemian.app?

In the spring of 2018, Google launched their .app top level domain extension, and I (@oyvindsabo) was quick to re-register steemian.app. At the time, I didn't know what I was going to use it for, but as time passed, I decided to develop a website which would provide useful writing tools for Steeians.

The spell checker

The spell checker is a work in progress writing tool, which aims to notify authors of common spelling mistakes, as well as grammatical mistakes. While the long term goal is to suggest fixes for nearly all writing mistakes, it currently only notifies about writing mistakes to which it has a suggested fix. It currently covers all mistakes which would otherwise be pointed out by the @grammarnazi bot.

Free illustrations

The free illustrations search engine aims to provide Steemians with free illustrations to use in their posts, without the need for including credits to the author. All the illustrations are drawn by me (@oyvindsabo). The long term goal of this offer is to provide images representing a lot of dictionary keywords, to achieve a continuously growing amount of search results linking to Steemit posts. My hope is that this will introduce more users to the amazing Steem ecosystem.

Why the need to login?

To support the creation and maintenance of the site, an upvote is automatically given by the user every time the user clicks a button on the site, given that at least 1000 seconds have passed since the last time such an automated upvote occurred.
